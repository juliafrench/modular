Offering a superlight build, directional shape and powder rocker profile for maximum versatility in all snow conditions, Century 109 skis are a fantastic all-mountain-friendly powder ski for women.

  • Step Down Sidewall 2.0 ensures maximum torsional rigidity, edge grip and controlled tip-to-tail flex
  • Carbon Sprocket Power Booster (SPB) offers maximum rocker stability
  • Lite wood core provides smooth handling at high speeds and in deep powder
  • Powder Rocker provides maximum flotation and playfulness in deep powder snow

Imported.

Bindings Installation

Bindings installation is not included with your purchase.
